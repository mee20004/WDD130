Client Report

As a client, I decided to get a website based on a photo gallery because I thought it would be easy to create. My brother is a photographer, and I got my photos from some of his galleries. I communicated with my developer, giving her any resources she needed, like the logo for the site. Overall, the developers did not reach my expectations for the site. I designed my site using Figma—it was very clear and simple for them to create—but the home page looks nothing like the design, and the other two pages still don’t exist at all. Next time I hire a developer to create a website, I would hire someone who is better at creating websites.

General Experience and Reflections

The most challenging part for me was creating a site with the worst colors I have ever seen. I overcame this by trying to use the bright, differing colors sparingly and focusing on the gray and red. The part I enjoyed most was doing the HTML; it was nice and easy to put together and was stress-free compared to the design. If I were to do the project again, I would contact the client and encourage him to choose a different color scheme. Overall, I don’t think I necessarily feel more confident in working on real-world projects, but I do feel more confident in my own HTML and CSS skills.
